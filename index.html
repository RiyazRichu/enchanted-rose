Happy Rose Day

In a garden where moonlight glows,

Bloomed a rose that softly shows.

Not just petals, red and bright,

But a heart that brings the light.

Her fragrance drifts in silent air,

A whisper gentle, kind and rare.

Though the winds may rise and call,

She stands with grace, above them all.

Not just beauty, rich and deep,

But secrets in her soul she keeps.

A bloom of warmth, a spark divine,

A rose that fate made only mine.

Happy Rose Day my Princess.
